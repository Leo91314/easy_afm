## 快速检查命令（复现前常用）
```bash
cd red-panda-afm
rg --files
rg -n "esp32doit-devkit-v1|monitor_speed" firmware/platformio.ini
rg -n "processCommand|controlTask|scan|approach|pid_control" firmware/src/main.cpp
rg -n "start_scan|export_tiff|_serial_read_loop" gui/afm.py
```
